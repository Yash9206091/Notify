Your trust is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Notify.

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

Thank you for using Notify, your smart assistant for last-minute exam preparation. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

a. Personal Information

  • Name: For account identification and personalization.
  • Email Address / Phone Number: Used for login, communication, and notifications.
  • Institutional Details: (e.g., college name, department, year) if you choose to share them.

b. Usage Data

  • Notes created, edited, or deleted
  • Subjects accessed or searched
  • Login timestamps and device type
  • Analytics related to app performance (using tools like Firebase Analytics)

c. Communication Data

Messages or discussions shared via any in-app collaboration or chat tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to:

  • Provide and maintain the application services
  • Enable secure login and user authentication
  • Save and sync your notes across devices
  • Send notifications or reminders (e.g., exam alerts)
  • Analyze user engagement and improve user experience
  • Fix bugs and detect/prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share data:

  • With third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase) strictly for hosting, analytics, and authentication.
  • When legally required, to comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Use, or protect the rights and safety of Notify and its users.

4. Data Security

We take data protection seriously. All data is:

  • Encrypted in transit (via HTTPS)
  • Stored securely using industry best practices (e.g., Firebase Firestore with Firestore Security Rules)
  • Access-controlled based on user authentication and authorization

However, no system is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Edit or delete your notes or account data
  • Request permanent deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of non-essential notifications

You can manage these through your user settings or by contacting us at notifyapp@gmail.com.
